Create an account

Use this service to create a new My NHS Pension account. The registration process takes just a few minutes.

During the registration you will be asked to confirm a number of key items of personal information. This information is used to validate your identity against the information that is held on our system.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • your registration code
  • your personal identification code
  • your member number
  • your National Insurance number
