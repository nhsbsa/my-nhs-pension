How to find your information

Member number

An 8 digit code, for example 78364736

You may be able to find it on your:

  • payslip, listed as 'SD ref'
  • current Total Rewards Statement (TRS), listed as 'member number'
  • current Annual Benefit Statement (ABS), listed as 'member number'

National Insurance number

Made up of 2 letters, 6 numbers and a final letter, for example QQ 12 34 56 A

You may be able to find it on your:

  • payslip
  • P60
  • letters about tax, pensions or benefits

For more information visit the Find a lost National Insurance number page.
